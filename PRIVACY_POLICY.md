# Privacy Policy — MetaFactory: Meta AI Automation

**Last updated: April 12, 2026**

## Overview

MetaFactory is a Chrome extension that automates bulk prompt submission on Meta AI (meta.ai) and downloads generated images to your local device. This policy explains what data the extension accesses and how it is handled.

## Data Collection

**MetaFactory does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

The extension operates entirely on your local device and communicates only between its own components (the side panel and the Meta AI tab you have open).

## Data Accessed

### Prompts you type
- Your text prompts are read from the extension's side panel and sent directly to the Meta AI tab in your browser.
- Prompts are never sent to any server other than Meta AI itself (which you are already using directly).
- Prompts are not stored beyond your current browsing session.

### Tab URLs
- The extension reads the URL of the active tab to verify you are on `https://www.meta.ai` before enabling automation.
- URLs are checked locally and are never logged, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

### Generated images
- Images generated by Meta AI are downloaded directly to your local Downloads folder using the Chrome `downloads` API.
- No images are uploaded to or processed by any external server.

### Language preference
- Your selected language (EN, Hindi, Punjabi, or French) is saved locally using `chrome.storage.local`.
- This preference stays on your device and is never transmitted externally.

## Permissions Used

| Permission | Why it is needed |
|---|---|
| `activeTab` | Verify the current tab is on meta.ai before running |
| `tabs` | Detect tab switches to keep the UI status accurate |
| `scripting` | Inject the automation script into the Meta AI tab |
| `downloads` | Save generated images to your local Downloads folder |
| `sidePanel` | Display the extension's control panel |
| `storage` | Save your language preference locally |
| `host_permissions: meta.ai/*` | Operate only on Meta AI pages — no other sites |

## Third-Party Services

MetaFactory does not integrate with, communicate with, or send data to any third-party service or analytics platform.

All automation happens between your Chrome browser and Meta AI (meta.ai), which is a service you are using independently. Meta AI's own privacy policy governs how Meta handles your prompts and generated images on their platform.

## Data Retention

No data is retained by MetaFactory. The extension holds your prompts in memory only for the duration of the current automation run. Once the run completes or you close the panel, no data persists.

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. It does not knowingly collect data from any users.
